Portfolio Overview

Client: Meat Junction

Occassionally the agency will collaborate with other agencies on a project, and this was such a project.

We were excited to collaborate on a food styling and food photography project for Meat Junction franchise in Durban, South Africa. The franchise located in 3 locations: Durban CBD and Richard’s Bay. The project centred around ‘steamed’ bread which was a core offering at the restaurant. The bread has been in the African food staple and offers nostalgic indulgence from its main customers.

The agency wias tasked to increase its creative asset library for the restaurant, social media and website.

project overview

The agency partnered with Violynne Communications to shoot food photography for Meat Junction franchise in Durban. The brand was keen on insighing nostalgia from existing consumers for traditional isiZulu food, while also capturing the colorful vibrancyin images that will tantalise the taste buds.

Food styling and art direction was critical to the output in the photography. The client was extremely satisfied with the tone and mood presented from the images we had captured.
