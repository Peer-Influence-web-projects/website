Portfolio Overview

Client: Grande Cosmetics for MyCherryBlossomBeauty

International beauty brands face challenges with local meritocracy, and this was also apparent for global brand Grande Cosmetics, a beauty brand sold by e-retailer, MyCherryBlossomBeauty.

Peer & Influence agency set about a beauty content collaboration project to create awareness within South African community.

Peer & Influence (P&I), set developing beauty content collaboration aimed to create awareness to the growing community on P&I Instagram page.

P&I conceptualised a social media campaign to showcase the vibrancy of the brand lipsticks on African skintones. The creative assets were developed as part of a collabo and to feature on the new P&I Instagram page.

Our Key Focus

Increase Mental availability

Extend brand to local community

Create Social Media engagement

Results Achieved

  • increased awareness of brand

  • 1500+ content engagment on social

  • increased traffic to brand social media
